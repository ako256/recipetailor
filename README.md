# Recipe Tailor – Your Smart AI Recipe Assistant

**Recipe Tailor** is an AI-powered recipe generator that dynamically creates personalized recipes based on the ingredients you have.  
From meal planning to calorie tracking, Recipe Tailor helps you cook smarter, waste less, and eat better!

---

## Features

-  **AI-Generated Recipes** – Get multiple creative recipe ideas instantly, customized to your chosen ingredients.  
- **Smart Shopping List** – Automatically generate shopping lists from your selected recipes.  
- **Save & Like Recipes** – Keep track of your favorite dishes for easy access later.  
- **Weekly Meal Planner** – Plan your meals for the entire week and stay organized.  
- **Calorie Tracking** – View estimated calorie counts for each meal to help you maintain your dietary goals.
